Adversaries

The Main Adversaries are as follows

  1. "Bad" Medical (e.g. Dr. Galal Badrawy, Dr. Raju Bangaru (PDF), Dr. Yolande Ferguson and Sinéad Frain).
  2. "Bad" Freemasons (e.g. Manu Gambhir, Andrew Caras-Altas, Ana Aldecoa Torreira (GoldPlay UK, London, 2002), Dr. Galal Badrawy, Dr. Yolande Ferguson, Sinéad Frain and Nicky/Nicholas Moynihan (www.TheBosun.ie, Co. Cork, 2022)).
  3. MI6/SIS (e.g. Ana Aldecoa Torreira (GoldPlay UK, London, 2002), Dr. Galal Badrawy, Dr. Yolande Ferguson, Sinéad Frain and Nicky/Nicholas Moynihan (www.TheBosun.ie, Co. Cork, 2022)).
  4. MI5 (Web Site) / MI5 (Local Page) (e.g. Elizabeth Lydia Manningham-Buller).
  5. GCHQ.

"Bad" Medical

Arguably the most Dangerous of the Adversaries. They should never be underestimated and should be treated with caution and ultimately a certain amount of respect for their power and abilities to affect all Entities.

They appear to have access to Impressive Advances in Medical and other Technologies that should be used for the betterment of Mankind, rather than Subjugating them, which appears to be their forté.

One weakness may be an ability to Target only a select few Entities at a time, so the more Entities who resist them, the harder a time the "Bad" Medical will have.

Another weakness may be a reliance/dependence on the British Security Services to provide Surveillance, Intelligence, Anlaysis et al. to them in order for them to fully function. As the British Security Services come under increased pressure from the CIA and the NSA this weakness may well become an Achilles Heel.

Other weaknesses may be Low Emotional Intelligence (EQ), including Low to Non-Existent Empathy and/or Compassion, and a propensity to Enormous Ego and Arrogance, resulting in them being unable or unwilling to NOT reveal themselves in what they believe to be a show of force/strength.

"Bad" Freemasons

Arguably the Second most Dangerous of the Adversaries, especially when they liaise with the "Bad" Medical. The "Bad" Freemasons appear to have enough Financial Resources and Clout to corrupt Entities Worldwide. They can even influence and/or corrupt British Security Services. Very few Entities can resist the attraction of Financial Resources offered by the "Bad" Freemasons, so again they should never be underestimated, and again should be treated with caution and ultimately a certain amount of respect.

Weaknesses may be Low Emotional Intelligence (EQ), including Low to Non-Existent Empathy and/or Compassion, and a propensity to Enormous Ego and Arrogance, resulting in them being unable or unwilling to NOT reveal themselves in what they believe to be a show of force/strength.

British Security Services

This Includes:

  1. MI6/SIS.
  2. MI5 (Web Site) / MI5 (Local Page).
  3. GCHQ.

According to potential indications from the NSA this MAY NOT Include (although this is not certain):

  1. NCSC.

The British Security Services appear to be a particluarly covert, cowardly, insidious and vile sub-human species.

One weakness may be the that they have become Marginalised and increasingly Irrelevant by Corruption by the "Bad" Medical and the "Bad" Freemasons. Additionally, without the assistance of the "Bad" Medical in particular, their power and effectiveness is diminished significantly.

Another emerging weakness may be the realisation that the CIA and the NSA will ultimately replace them as the Central Security Service Globally.

Other weaknesses may be Low Emotional Intelligence (EQ), including Low to Non-Existent Empathy and/or Compassion, and a propensity to Enormous Ego and Arrogance, resulting in them being unable or unwilling to NOT reveal themselves in what they believe to be a show of force/strength. It should be noted that some may possess significantly above average Intelligence (IQ), which should not be confused with Emotional Intelligence (EQ).
